## Overview

Lumenboard offers a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plan purchases. This policy applies to both new subscriptions and plan upgrades.

## 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your Lumenboard subscription for any reason, you may request a full refund within 14 calendar days of the initial charge date. The 14-day window begins on the date your payment is processed, not the date you first log in.

Refunds under the 14-day guarantee are full refunds of the amount charged — no questions asked.

To request a refund, email billing@lumenboard.io with your account email address and the reason for cancellation (optional but appreciated for improving our product).

## Pro-Rated Refunds After 14 Days

After the 14-day guarantee period, Lumenboard does not issue full refunds. However, we do offer pro-rated credits for unused time when a subscription is cancelled mid-cycle under the following conditions:

- **Annual subscriptions cancelled after 14 days**: Customers who cancel an annual subscription after the 14-day window are eligible for a pro-rated credit for the remaining full months of the subscription term. Partial months are not credited. Credits are applied to the Lumenboard account and may be used toward any future Lumenboard plan.
- **Monthly subscriptions cancelled after 14 days**: No refund or credit is issued for the current billing month. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

### Pro-Rated Credit Calculation

For annual subscriptions, the credit is calculated as:

```
credit = (annual_charge / 12) × remaining_full_months
```

For example, a customer on the Pro annual plan ($276/year = $23/month) who cancels after 5 months would receive a credit for 7 remaining full months: 7 × $23 = $161.

## Exceptions and Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds or credits:

- Add-on purchases (e.g., extra data retention, premium integrations sold separately)
- Fees for professional services or onboarding assistance
- Charges that have already been credited or discounted

## Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback with your payment provider before contacting Lumenboard support, your account will be suspended pending resolution. We strongly recommend contacting billing@lumenboard.io first — most disputes are resolved within one business day.

## How to Cancel

Cancel your subscription at any time from the **Account Settings → Billing** page. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation before being deleted.
