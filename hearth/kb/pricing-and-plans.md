## Overview

Lumenboard offers three subscription tiers: Free, Pro, and Team. All plans include access to the core dashboard builder. Pricing is per-seat, billed monthly or annually (annual billing saves 20%).

## Free Plan

The Free plan is available to all users at no cost.

- **Price**: $0/month
- **Seats**: 1 (owner only)
- **Dashboards**: Up to 3
- **Data sources**: 2 connected integrations
- **Data retention**: 30 days of query history
- **CSV export**: Up to 500 rows per export
- **Support**: Community forums only

Free plan users cannot invite collaborators or access the API.

## Pro Plan

The Pro plan is designed for individual power users and small teams.

- **Price**: $29/month per seat (monthly billing) or $23/month per seat (annual billing)
- **Seats**: 1–5
- **Dashboards**: Unlimited
- **Data sources**: Up to 10 connected integrations
- **Data retention**: 12 months of query history
- **CSV export**: Up to 100,000 rows per export; scheduled exports available
- **API access**: Full REST API with rate limit of 1,000 requests/hour
- **Support**: Email support with 24-hour response SLA

## Team Plan

The Team plan is built for larger organizations needing collaboration and governance features.

- **Price**: $79/month per seat (monthly billing) or $63/month per seat (annual billing); minimum 5 seats
- **Seats**: 5 or more (no cap)
- **Dashboards**: Unlimited with version history
- **Data sources**: Unlimited integrations
- **Data retention**: 36 months of query history
- **CSV export**: Unlimited rows; real-time streaming export available
- **API access**: Full REST API with rate limit of 10,000 requests/hour; webhook support
- **Support**: Priority email and live chat; 4-hour response SLA for P1 issues
- **SSO**: SAML 2.0 and OIDC supported
- **Audit logs**: Full admin audit trail with 90-day retention

## Plan Upgrades and Downgrades

Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated to the current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. When downgrading, data and dashboards exceeding the lower plan's limits are placed in read-only mode for 30 days before being archived.

## Enterprise Pricing

Organizations requiring more than 50 seats, custom SLAs, or on-premise deployment should contact sales@lumenboard.io for a custom quote.
